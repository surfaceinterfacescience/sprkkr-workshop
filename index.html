Workshop on Electron Spectroscopy and Hands-On Training in the Multiple-Scattering-Based SPR-KKR Code and Related Applications (Hybrid Mode)


Want to take part?

Prof. Jan Minar from the University of West Bohemia - New Technologies Research Center, Pilsen, Czech Republic, and our Surface and Interface Science group from IIT Bombay will jointly conduct a hands-on workshop for the Munich SPR-KKR band structure program package code usage at IIT Bombay.

If you are interested in participating, feel free to fill the google form.

The workshop is organized with the support and sponsorship of IIT Bombay.
